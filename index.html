Abstract

This project explores the application of 3D and 4D Gaussian Splatting techniques to convert exocentric views into photorealistic egocentric perspectives. It addresses the challenges of spatial transformation, occlusion, and viewpoint discrepancy in tasks like VR, robotics, and AR. Our method outperforms baseline models (e.g., Pix2Pix, P-GAN) on metrics like SSIM, PSNR, and LPIPS.
