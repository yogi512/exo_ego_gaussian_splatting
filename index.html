Abstract

This project investigates the task of translating exo-centric video footage into photorealistic ego-centric views using 3D and 4D Gaussian Splatting. It aims to bridge the gap between third-person and first-person perspectives for applications in virtual reality, robotics, and surveillance. Our pipeline reconstructs dynamic 3D scenes using COLMAP and Gaussian rendering and achieves superior results over baselines like Pix2Pix and P-GAN in terms of SSIM (0.76), PSNR (23.35), and LPIPS (0.26).
